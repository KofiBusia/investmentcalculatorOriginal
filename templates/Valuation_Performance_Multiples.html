Understanding Valuation Metrics

These metrics assess a company’s valuation and financial performance for investment analysis.

Low Metrics

e.g., P/E < 15x, ROE < 10%

May indicate undervaluation or lower performance.

High Metrics

e.g., P/E > 25x, ROE > 20%

May indicate high growth or strong performance.

How to Use

  1. Select Metric

    Choose a metric like P/E or ROE.

  2. Enter Data

    Input required financial data for the year.

  3. Calculate

    Click “Calculate” to see results and interpretation.

Download Guide Watch Tutorial

To calculate these metrics, gather data from:

  • Financial Statements: Balance sheet (equity, assets), income statement (net income, EBITDA).
  • Market Data: Share price, market cap from Yahoo Finance or gse.com.gh.
  • Analyst Estimates: EPS growth from Yahoo Finance “Analysis” tab.
