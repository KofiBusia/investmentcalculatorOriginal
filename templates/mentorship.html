How It Works

Simple. Structured. Results-driven.

1

Apply

Fill in the application form below with your goals and background. Takes under 3 minutes.

2

Get Matched

Our team reviews your profile and matches you with the best available mentor in your chosen area.

3

Kickoff Session

Meet your mentor for an intro call. Set goals, agree on a schedule, and define your 90-day roadmap.

4

Grow & Achieve

Monthly sessions, accountability check-ins, and opportunity referrals. We track your progress with you.

Who Should Apply?

University students (any year) in finance, business, economics, accounting, or related fields
Recent graduates seeking their first role in capital markets, banking, or corporate finance
Students seeking internship or national service placement in the financial sector
Young professionals (1–3 years experience) looking to accelerate and transition into new roles
Members of the Young Investors Network seeking deeper professional development
Anyone preparing for CFA, ACCA, or ICAG qualifications who wants structured support
