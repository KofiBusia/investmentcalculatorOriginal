About Forex Trading

Foreign exchange (forex) trading involves buying and selling currencies to profit from changes in exchange rates. This calculator computes the profit or loss from a forex trade based on the investment amount and the initial and final exchange rates between two currencies.

Input Fields

Provide the following details to calculate your forex trade outcome:

  • Investment Amount: The amount invested in the base currency.
  • Initial Exchange Rate: The exchange rate at the start of the trade (e.g., 1.2 for 1 USD = 1.2 EUR).
  • Final Exchange Rate: The exchange rate at the end of the trade.
