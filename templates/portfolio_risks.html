How to Use the Portfolio Risk Calculator

Follow these steps to assess portfolio risk compliant with NPRA guidelines.

  1. Select Risk Metric

    Choose a risk metric from the dropdown (e.g., Volatility, Sharpe Ratio, VaR).

  2. Enter Portfolio Allocations

    Input percentage allocations for each asset class, ensuring compliance with NPRA limits.

  3. Provide Market and Portfolio Data

    Enter portfolio value, market return, volatility, benchmark return, peak, and trough values.

  4. Calculate and Review

    Click “Calculate” to compute the selected risk metric and review the results.
