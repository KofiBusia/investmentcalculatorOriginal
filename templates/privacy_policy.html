InvestIQ ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy under the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843). This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you use InvestIQ:

We do not collect card numbers, bank account numbers, or full MoMo wallet details. Payment references are used solely to verify manual MoMo transfers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide our services — managing your account, CV builder, job matching, training bookings, and access to practice question sections.
  • To process access fee payments — verifying your MoMo payment reference and issuing an access code.
  • To communicate with you — sending access codes, booking confirmations, and service updates to the email address you provide.
  • To improve the platform — analysing anonymised usage patterns to enhance features.
  • To comply with legal obligations — retaining records as required by applicable Ghanaian law.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing (Act 843, Section 17)

We process your personal data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance — to deliver the service you have requested (e.g., issuing a practice questions access code after payment).
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the platform.
  • Consent — where you have affirmatively opted in (e.g., employer data sharing in the CV builder).
  • Legal obligation — where retention is required by Ghanaian law.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With employers (CV builder only) — if you have explicitly opted in to the employer matching programme, your CV data may be shared with vetted partner employers.
  • With our email service provider — Gmail (Google) is used to send you transactional emails. Google's privacy policy applies to data transmitted via their servers.
  • With our hosting provider — Render.com hosts our servers and database. Render's data processing terms apply.
  • As required by law — we will disclose data if required by a valid court order or legal obligation under Ghanaian law.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained for as long as your account is active, plus 2 years after closure.
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping obligations.
  • CV and job application data — retained for up to 2 years, or until you request deletion.
  • Session data — expires after 24 hours of inactivity.

6. Your Rights (Act 843, Section 18)

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act 2012, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at kyeikofi@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies and Sessions

We use server-side session cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your practice question access. These cookies are functional and necessary for the platform to operate. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can clear session cookies by logging out or clearing your browser's cookie data.

8. Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), server-side session management, and restricted admin access. No internet transmission is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we treat data security as a priority obligation.

9. Children

InvestIQ is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us immediately for deletion.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of InvestIQ after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
