InvestIQ Privacy Policy

investright.onrender.com  ·  InvestIQ Mobile App

Last updated: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

InvestIQ ("we", "our", or "us") operates the InvestIQ mobile application and the website at investright.onrender.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By using InvestIQ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use InvestIQ:

  • Account information: Full name and email address when you register
  • CV / profile data: Job title, skills, availability, location, LinkedIn URL, and professional summary (only when you choose to submit a CV profile)
  • Job applications: Name, email, phone number, and cover letter when you apply for a job
  • Training bookings: Name, email, phone, and company name when you book a training programme
  • Employer inquiries: Name, email, company, and message when an employer contacts a candidate
  • Usage data: Anonymous app usage information to improve the service

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your CV profile to employers (only if you opt in)
  • To process job applications and training bookings
  • To send confirmations and relevant communications
  • To improve and personalise the InvestIQ experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Employers: If you opt in to the talent directory, employers can see your name, role, skills, and availability. Your email and phone remain private until you choose to respond.
  • Service providers: We use Render.com for hosting. They process data solely to provide our service.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted passwords and secure HTTPS connections. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. opt out of the talent directory)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

8. Children's Privacy

InvestIQ is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of InvestIQ after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
